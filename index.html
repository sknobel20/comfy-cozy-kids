Why safety-net hospitals

The big-name hospitals already have national donors, huge fundraisers, and corporate sponsors. Safety-net hospitals, the ones that treat low-income and uninsured families in our own neighborhoods, often don't. That's where we focus. A handmade card or a small fundraiser goes a long way when nobody else is showing up.
