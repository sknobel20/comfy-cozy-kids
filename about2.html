Our story

Every child deserves a nurturing environment.

At BLS Comfy Cozy Kids, our mission is to support safety-net children's hospitals by providing comfort and joy to young patients and their families. While many organizations focus on large, well-known hospitals, we work to uplift local hospitals that serve their communities but often receive less attention and resources.

BLS Comfy Cozy Kids was inspired by a club of the same name started at Lexington High School. We're not a formal chapter or branch, just a separately run club at Boston Latin School inspired by the same idea of supporting hospitalized children.

We believe that every child deserves a nurturing environment no matter their circumstances, and we are dedicated to making a positive impact through collaboration and compassion.

Why safety-net hospitals

The big-name hospitals already have national donors, huge fundraisers, and corporate sponsors. Safety-net hospitals, the ones that treat low-income and uninsured families in our own neighborhoods, often don't. That's where we focus. A handmade card or a small fundraiser goes a long way when nobody else is showing up.

Franciscan Children's

Our first fundraiser raised over $300 to support patient care and family programs at this Brighton-based pediatric hospital. This past year we added a bake sale, bringing our total to over $400.

Boston Medical Center

Our volunteers handcrafted 50 seasonal cards and delivered them to young patients over the winter. We made 50 more this spring and are coordinating our next delivery window.
